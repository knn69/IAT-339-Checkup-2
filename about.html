Our Story

Our story began in 2010 with humble beginnings as a family run mom and pop corner shop. Throughout progressing years, our business platforms started to expand through e-commerce in light of providing a more convenient experience for our clientele. 2018 was shadowed by a robbery which affected us financially necessitating the sale of our physical location and moving solely to an e-commerce site. However, this time turned out to be one which saw vast growth in our partnerships through the setup of a new physical store and an upgrade to our e-commerce website. Slated for 2022, our plans for expansion include those of physical and digital grocery markets through new innovations within our online and physical shopping platforms. No matter what comes around the corner, we always think about our customers first!
